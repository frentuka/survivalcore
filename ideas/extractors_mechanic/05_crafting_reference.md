# Crafting & Items Reference

Complete crafting recipes for all items in the Extractor Mechanic.

---

## A. Compact Blocks

**Recipe:** 3×3 grid filled with the vanilla block.  
*E.g., 9 Blocks of Coal = 1 Compact Coal Block (which equals 81 Coal).*

**Applicable materials:** Coal, Iron, Gold, Diamond, Emerald, Redstone, Lapis, Copper.

**Reversible:** 1 Compact Block placed in the crafting grid yields 9 of the regular block.
